Career Overview Why Game Development? MIT Caltech

Game Development

Career Summary:

Game developers are artists, using their creativity to create a concept, and transform that concept into an experience. Like a painter, they use their scripts, sound-design, animation game engines, and such, as a canvas to express themselves and their creative vision. Depending if they are a leading developer in a team, an independent developer, or neither, they may have complete creative control over the finished product or just work on releasing the game, helping an experience come to life.

Salary

$108,471 per year according to Zip Recruiter (December 2023).

Education Typically Needed:

A Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science is typically needed.

Main Skills Needed (Optional only required when working as independent or lead developers):

  • Basic programming knowledge (Minimum) (Usually in C++, C#)
  • Graphics (Optional) (Ex. Sprite-making, 3-D modeling)
  • Animation (Optional)
  • Sound-design (Optional)
  • Writing plot and characters (Optional, if needed)
